CONTACT FORM SETUP INSTRUCTIONS
===============================

The contact form is currently in "Simulation Mode". It shows a success message but DOES NOT send emails because it needs your personal EmailJS keys.

TO ENABLE REAL EMAILS (Takes 2 minutes):

1.  Register for free at: https://www.emailjs.com/
2.  Login and click "Add New Service" -> select "Gmail".
    *   This gives you a SERVICE_ID (e.g., service_abc123).
3.  Click "Email Templates" -> "Create New Template".
    *   This gives you a TEMPLATE_ID (e.g., template_xyz789).
4.  Click on your name/avatar in the top right -> "Account" -> "API Keys" -> "Public Key".
    *   This gives you a PUBLIC_KEY (e.g., user_12345).

ONCE YOU HAVE THE KEYS:

1.  Open correct file: c:\NSCHOOL\PORTFOLIO\src\components\Contact.jsx
2.  Find lines 14-36 inside the `sendEmail` function.
3.  Delete the simulation code and uncomment the real code.
4.  Replace the placeholders with your new keys.

EXAMPLE OF FINAL CODE:
----------------------
const SERVICE_ID = 'service_abc123';
const TEMPLATE_ID = 'template_xyz789';
const PUBLIC_KEY = 'user_12345';

emailjs.sendForm(SERVICE_ID, TEMPLATE_ID, form.current, PUBLIC_KEY)
   .then(() => {
       setIsSent(true);
       e.target.reset();
   }, (error) => {
       alert("Failed...");
   });
----------------------

After saving, the form will send real emails to your Gmail!
